Over the last several decades, a huge amount of climate data from numerous sources has been collected. This data is freely available to the public, but making sense of this vast amount of data is not easy!
By developing interactive visualizations and dashboards, we aim to educate the general public on the impact of climate change while motivating action through data-driven storytelling.
